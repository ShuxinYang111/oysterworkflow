# Code of Conduct

OysterWorkflow welcomes practical, technical, and critical feedback.

Please keep issues and discussions respectful, specific, and useful:

- critique ideas, behavior, artifacts, and product decisions rather than people
- do not post private credentials, customer data, account details, or sensitive screenshots
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, spam, or repeated off-topic promotion
- disclose relevant affiliation when recommending tools, services, or commercial workflows

Maintainers may edit or remove comments that expose sensitive data, derail issue triage, or make the repository less useful for early users and testers.

For sensitive licensing, commercial, or privacy questions, contact `shuxin.y.97@gmail.com`.
